## UK English Spelling

All user-facing content MUST use British/UK English spelling. This includes page copy, component text, blog content, CTAs, FAQs, meta descriptions, and any visible strings.

### Key rules

- **-ise not -ize:** minimise, maximise, specialise, sanitise, organise, realise, utilise, optimise, recognise, customise, revitalise, stabilise, neutralise, deodorise
- **-our not -or:** colour, odour, favour, honour, behaviour, neighbour, labour, humour
- **-re not -er:** centre, fibre, litre, metre
- **-isation not -ization:** sanitisation, neutralisation, specialisation, organisation
- **-ogue not -og:** catalogue, dialogue
- **Doubled L:** travelling, cancelling, labelling, modelling, signalling, levelling
- **mould** not mold
- **grey** not gray
- **kerb** not curb (e.g. "kerb appeal")
- **tyre** not tire
- **deionised** not deionized
- **enquiry** not inquiry (for general questions)
- **licence** (noun) not license
- **programme** (non-computing) not program
- **neighbourhood** not neighborhood
- **storey** (building floor) not story
- **speciality** not specialty
- **draught** not draft (non-technical)
- **plough** not plow
- **cheque** not check (financial)
- **deodoriser** not deodorizer
